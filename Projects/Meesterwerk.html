Digital Portfolio of Bart Koning

In Dutch high schools, students need to make a big final project in their last year. For this project, I choose to make a video on an existing song. For this video, I chose the song "Smells Like Teen Spirit" by Tommee Profitt - Nirvana, as I really enjoyed the remix he made of this classic song. For the project, I made drum sensors to clip onto drums, so that I could trigger lights with drum hits. I also made a lightshow for the song on my Launchpad. Once everything was prepared, I got a garage near my house to filming the whole project. My dad filmed the whole thing, while I played the drums and controlled the Launchpad. I finally edited the video in DaVinci Resolve, which took a LOT of time.
